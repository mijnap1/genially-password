About

This site started as a quick solution for two friends who constantly needed Genially passwords from our math class. Instead of sending them one by one every time, I figured I’d make my life easier and build a simple tool that connects to the Genially API and fetches all the passwords at once. I wanted it to look clean, feel smooth, and actually be helpful for students. Feel free to use it however you like, and if you’re curious about how it works behind the scenes, check out my GitHub page.
